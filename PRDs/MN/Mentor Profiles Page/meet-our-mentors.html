Meet Our Mentors

Experienced. Vetted. Certified. Always Free.

Professionals Who Have Been Where You Are

Our volunteer mentors are seasoned business leaders, executives, and entrepreneurs who give their time freely to help you succeed. Most bring prior mentoring experience from nationally recognized programs, including:

  • SCORE
  • I-Corps
  • Sea Change
  • Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses

Before working with clients, every CBM mentor is carefully vetted for their real-world business experience and completes our proprietary LISTEN certification program — ensuring you receive consistent, high-quality, client-centered guidance from day one.

The LISTEN Certification

All CBM mentors complete our LISTEN training — a proven methodology that puts your goals at the center of every session.

L
Listen
I
Inspire
S
Share
T
Trust
E
Expect
N
Navigate

Every mentor asks open-ended questions, shares relevant experience, maintains complete confidentiality, and ends every session with clear next steps — because your success is the only measure that matters.
